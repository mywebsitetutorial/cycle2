Frequently Asked Questions

What types of psychological services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of psychological services, including therapy for anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship issues, and more. You can find a detailed list of our services on our website.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office at 444 222 000 or by filling out the contact form on our website.

What are your fees?

Our fees vary depending on the type of service you need. We accept most major insurance plans. You can find more information about our fees on our website or by contacting our office.

Do you offer online therapy?

Yes, we offer online therapy through a secure platform. This allows you to receive therapy from the comfort of your own home.

What are your qualifications?

Our team of therapists is highly qualified and experienced. They are all licensed and certified in their respective fields.

What if I have a specific concern that isn't listed here?

Please feel free to contact us with any questions you may have. We are happy to assist you in any way we can.

What are your hours of operation?

Our office hours are Monday - Saturday from 9.00 am to 6.00 pm. You can also find more information on our website.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please provide at least one day notice if you need to cancel your appointment.

How do I find a therapist who is a good fit for me?

We can help you find a therapist who specializes in the areas you need and who is a good fit for your personality and preferences.

What is the difference between therapy and counseling?

Therapy and counseling are often used interchangeably. They both involve working with a mental health professional to address emotional, behavioral, or mental health concerns.
