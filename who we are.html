Who We Are

The professionals at Therpay Clinic are dedicated professionals on a mission to serve the mental well-being of our community. Based on the concepts of compassion, integrity, and excellence, our organization strives to treat individuals and families presenting a wide array of concerns. We believe that everyone deserves the best possible care, and thus, we have a myriad of available services to meet each client's needs in unique ways. This is provided by our team of experienced psychologists and therapists along with support staff who work together to offer a safe place that is as comfortable as possible. At ABC Care, we offer more than service provision; we partner with you on this journey of recovery for better mental health and a brighter future.
