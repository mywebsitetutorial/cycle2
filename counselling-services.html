Counselling Services

The counseling services from Therpay Clinic provide a platform that is comfortable and empathetic, where one can discuss all one's personal problems and emotional issues. Our efficient counsellors work together to help the clients through various issues: relationships, bereavement, self-confidence building, adjustment to life, and many more. We follow a client-centered approach; your experience and needs will be the guide to counseling. We are here to offer you clarity, coping skills, and resilience through compassionate listening and evidence-based strategies. Whether you are looking for short-term guidance or ongoing support, our aim is to empower you to take on obstacles and live your life more fully.
